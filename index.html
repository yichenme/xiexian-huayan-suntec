Corporate Brand & Culture

Singapore Oriental Shenhua Catering Services Pte. Ltd. brings Chinese banquet culture into a Singapore landmark setting.

Singapore Oriental Shenhua Catering Services Pte. Ltd. is the operating company behind the proposed Singapore flagship. The company is built around the idea of presenting contemporary Chinese dining as a complete cultural experience: cuisine, hospitality, art, private-room etiquette, and ceremonial banquets.

Brand Culture Statement

The brand takes inspiration from China’s tradition of refined banquets, seasonal ingredients, hospitality rituals, and poetic visual culture. In Singapore, the aim is to create a high-end Chinese dining venue that feels rooted in Chinese aesthetics while meeting international expectations for service, privacy, comfort, and operational discipline.

Project Overview

Shanghai-origin premium Chinese banquet dining, launched in Singapore.

Crab Art Banquet is a self-owned brand operated in Singapore by Singapore Oriental Shenhua Catering Services Pte. Ltd., planning its first Singapore outlet as a landmark flagship restaurant. The proposed concept is designed for high-end banquets, business entertainment, private dining, and premium Chinese culinary experiences.

Brand Identity

Crab Art Banquet is positioned as a high-end Chinese art restaurant, originating from Shanghai and built around refined crab banquet cuisine, Chinese aesthetics, and elevated hospitality.

Singapore Flagship

The Singapore outlet will be the brand’s first store in Singapore and is intended to serve as a recognisable local flagship for Suntec City and the wider Marina Centre area.

Core Occasion

The restaurant will focus on business hosting, VIP private rooms, family celebrations, festive banquets, and high-value dining occasions.

Brand Positioning

A premium banquet destination, not a casual seafood restaurant.

The concept combines food, space, service, privacy, and ceremony into a complete dining experience suitable for Singapore’s high-end corporate and social market.

Product, Price & Space

Clear premium positioning for Singapore’s business and private dining market.

The offer is structured around high-ticket dining occasions, with product categories, price bands, and interior standards aligned to a flagship restaurant rather than a casual seafood outlet.

Product Categories

  • Signature crab banquet menus

  • VIP private-room menus

Price Positioning

Target average spend of S$300–S$400+ per guest, with higher-value private room and banquet spending during corporate hosting, festive periods, and VIP occasions.

Décor Direction

Contemporary Chinese luxury: warm wood, deep red and gold accents, curated art features, refined lighting, premium tableware, and private rooms designed for discreet hosting.

Market Rationale

Singapore has the right audience for a high-ticket Chinese banquet brand.

The market has a strong Chinese cultural foundation, large corporate and tourist flows, and proven acceptance for high-design, high-service restaurants. The concept is especially relevant for business dining, private social occasions, and premium Chinese hospitality.
