Project Overview

Shanghai-origin premium Chinese banquet dining, launched in Singapore.

Xiexian Huayan is a self-owned brand planning its first Singapore outlet as a landmark flagship restaurant. The proposed concept is designed for high-end banquets, business entertainment, private dining, and premium Chinese culinary experiences.

Brand Identity

Xiexian Huayan is positioned as a high-end Chinese art restaurant, originating from Shanghai and built around refined crab banquet cuisine, Chinese aesthetics, and elevated hospitality.

Singapore Flagship

The Singapore outlet will be the brand’s first store in the market and is intended to serve as a recognisable flagship, not a standard casual dining expansion.

Core Occasion

The restaurant will focus on business hosting, VIP private rooms, family celebrations, festive banquets, and high-value dining occasions.

Brand Positioning

A premium banquet destination, not a casual seafood restaurant.

The concept combines food, space, service, privacy, and ceremony into a complete dining experience suitable for Singapore’s high-end corporate and social market.

Core Pillars

1
Premium Chinese Cuisine
Signature crab banquets, seasonal seafood, refined Chinese set menus, and chef-led presentations.
2

Chinese-inspired interiors, curated art details, elegant lighting, premium tableware, and a strong sense of occasion.
3
High-End Hospitality
Private-room service, corporate hosting standards, curated menus, and a controlled guest experience.

Target Customers

  • Corporate clients and business entertainment guests
  • High-income local residents and Chinese-speaking communities
  • Regional visitors, international tourists, and convention guests
  • Families seeking premium celebration dining
  • VIP private dining and banquet customers
  • Guests seeking a refined Chinese dining landmark in central Singapore
Market Rationale

Singapore has the right audience for a high-ticket Chinese banquet brand.

The market has a strong Chinese cultural foundation, large corporate and tourist flows, and proven acceptance for high-design, high-service restaurants. The concept is especially relevant for business dining, private social occasions, and premium Chinese hospitality.

Chinese dining culture Business entertainment Private dining rooms Convention traffic Tourism & regional guests High average spend
Why Suntec City

A natural home for a landmark Chinese flagship.

Suntec City’s office catchment, convention connectivity, tourist accessibility, and established retail-dining environment make it highly suitable for a premium Chinese banquet and private dining destination.

Commercial Catchment

Direct access to office professionals, corporate hosts, and business guests supports weekday dinner, private room, and corporate banquet demand.

Convention & Visitor Flow

Proximity to events, exhibitions, hotels, and Marina Centre traffic creates strong relevance for regional and international guests.

Dining Mix Enhancement

The restaurant can add a distinctive premium Chinese art dining concept to Suntec City’s tenant mix, attracting higher-spending customers.

Site Requirement

Designed for privacy, comfort, and service flow.

The proposed restaurant will prioritise atmosphere and guest experience over maximum seat density, with a balanced mix of main dining and VIP rooms.

Investment & Returns

Serious flagship-level investment with long-term positioning.

To achieve a standard close to Shanghai Xiexian Huayan’s high-end positioning, the estimated project investment is approximately S$2.5 million to S$3.5 million.

Conclusion

Xiexian Huayan can become a premium Chinese dining landmark at Suntec City.

The Singapore flagship is designed as a high-end banquet and art dining destination with strong relevance to Suntec City’s commercial, convention, retail, and lifestyle ecosystem. It aims to attract high-spending local, corporate, regional, and tourist customers while strengthening the mall’s premium dining profile.
